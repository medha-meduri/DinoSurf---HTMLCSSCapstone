Meet the Brains behind Dinosurf!

Our founder, Dr. Sarah Johnson, is a renowned paleontologist with over 20 years of experience in the field. She has led numerous expeditions around the world and is passionate about sharing her knowledge and love of dinosaurs with others. We are a team of dinosaur enthusiasts dedicated to sharing our love of these magnificent creatures with the world. Our mission is to educate and inspire young and old alike about the wonders of paleontology and the importance of preserving our planet's history.
